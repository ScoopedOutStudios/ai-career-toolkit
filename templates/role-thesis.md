# Role Thesis

> This is the single most impactful file in the toolkit. Skills like
> **opportunity-evaluator** and **career-guide** reference it for every
> recommendation. Spend 10 minutes filling this out before your first prompt.
>
> `ai-career-toolkit init` will seed the Must-Haves and Non-Negotiables
> sections for you. Come back and fill in the rest when you're ready.

Define your target role, value proposition, and search criteria. This document guides opportunity evaluation, company targeting, and interview preparation.

## Target Role

- **Level:** <!-- The seniority you're targeting. Determines which rubric dimensions opportunity-evaluator emphasizes and how mock-interview-loop calibrates question difficulty. e.g., Senior, Staff, Sr Staff, Principal, Engineering Manager -->
- **Domains:** <!-- The technical areas you want to work in. Aligns with targeting.domains in settings.yaml for company list generation. e.g., platform engineering, AI/ML infrastructure, developer tools -->
- **Team type:** <!-- Helps career-guide assess role fit and growth trajectory. e.g., product team, platform team, founding team, growth-stage -->

- **Proof points:**
  <!-- 3-5 concrete achievements that back up your value proposition. star-story and mock-interview-loop will reference these for STAR narratives. -->

## Must-Haves

- **Compensation floor:** <!-- Minimum total comp. opportunity-evaluator uses this for the quick-filter pass/fail check. -->
- **Location / remote preferences:** <!-- Must match for a role to pass the quick filter. e.g., remote-first, hybrid in NYC, onsite OK in SF -->
- **Scope expectations:** <!-- What level of ownership you need. e.g., owning a domain, leading a team, IC with high autonomy -->

- **Industry:** <!-- Preferred industries. Informs target-list-generator tier scoring. -->
- **Product stage:** <!-- Helps career-guide evaluate growth opportunity. e.g., 0-to-1, scaling, mature optimization -->
- **Company stage:** <!-- Feeds into tier assignment. e.g., Series B+, growth-stage, public -->

## Non-Negotiables

- **Culture constraints:** <!-- Hard dealbreakers. opportunity-evaluator flags roles that violate these. e.g., no "move fast and break things" without reliability investment -->
- **Role constraints:** <!-- Structural dealbreakers. e.g., IC track with clear Staff+ progression, not a management role disguised as IC -->

## Quick Filter Checklist

Use this to rapidly screen new opportunities (score each 0 or 1):

- [ ] Level matches target
- [ ] Domain aligns with strengths
- [ ] Compensation meets floor
- [ ] Location/remote fits requirements
- [ ] Scope matches expectations
- [ ] Company stage is acceptable
- [ ] No non-negotiable violations
- [ ] Engineering culture signals are positive
- [ ] Growth trajectory is healthy
- [ ] Role advances career trajectory

**Score: ___/10** (8+ = pursue, 6-7 = investigate further, <6 = skip)
