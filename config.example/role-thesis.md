# Role Thesis — Example

This is an example of a filled-out role thesis. Copy to `config/role-thesis.md` or `~/.ai-career-toolkit/role-thesis.md` and replace with your own details.

## Target Role

- **Level:** Staff Engineer
- **Domains:** Platform engineering, developer tools, distributed systems
- **Team type:** Product-aligned platform team at a growth-stage or public company

## Value Proposition

- **What I uniquely bring:** Deep experience building reliable distributed systems with a track record of improving developer productivity. Strong at bridging the gap between platform capabilities and product team needs.
- **Proof points:**
  - Designed and shipped a service mesh migration that reduced inter-service latency by 35%
  - Built an internal deployment platform used by 40+ engineering teams
  - Led reliability improvements that moved core services from 99.9% to 99.99% availability

## Must-Haves

- **Compensation floor:** $250K total comp
- **Location / remote preferences:** Remote-first or hybrid in a major tech hub
- **Scope expectations:** Owning a domain with multi-team impact, not just feature work

## Nice-to-Haves

- **Industry:** Developer tools, cloud infrastructure, or AI/ML infrastructure
- **Product stage:** Scaling (product-market fit established, growing fast)
- **Company stage:** Series C+ or public

## Non-Negotiables

- **Culture constraints:** No "move fast and break things" without investment in reliability. Engineering culture that values craft.
- **Role constraints:** IC track with clear Staff+ progression. Not a management role disguised as IC.
