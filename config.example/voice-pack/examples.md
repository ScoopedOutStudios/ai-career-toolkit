# Voice Examples — Before / After

## Example 1: Resume Bullet

**Before (generic):**
> Spearheaded the development of a highly scalable microservices architecture that leveraged cutting-edge cloud technologies to deliver exceptional performance improvements across the organization.

**After (in voice):**
> Designed the microservices migration that cut API latency by 40% and eliminated the monthly scaling incidents the on-call team had been firefighting for a year.

**Why:** Specific outcome, no buzzwords, shows the human impact (on-call team), active voice.

## Example 2: LinkedIn Post

**Before (generic):**
> I'm thrilled to announce that I'm exploring new opportunities! I'm passionate about building amazing products and would love to connect with anyone who is hiring for senior engineering roles. Please don't hesitate to reach out!

**After (in voice):**
> I'm looking for my next role — Staff or Principal IC, focused on platform engineering or AI infrastructure. I'm especially interested in teams tackling hard systems problems where reliability and developer experience matter. If that sounds like your team, I'd welcome a conversation.

**Why:** Specific about what you want, no filler enthusiasm, clear CTA, respects the reader's time.

## Example 3: Recruiter Outreach Response

**Before (generic):**
> Thank you so much for reaching out! I am very interested in learning more about this exciting opportunity. I would love to schedule a call at your earliest convenience to discuss how my extensive experience could be a great fit for your team.

**After (in voice):**
> Thanks for the note. The role looks interesting — I'd like to learn more about the team's current technical challenges and where this role fits in the org. Happy to do a quick call this week. What works on your end?

**Why:** Direct, shows genuine curiosity about the substance, proposes a concrete next step.
