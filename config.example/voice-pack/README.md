# Voice Pack

Your personal voice pack defines how `in-my-voice` rewrites content to sound like you. Copy this directory to `config/voice-pack/` or `~/.ai-career-toolkit/voice-pack/` and customize each file.

## Files

- **rulebook.md** — Core voice rules: what to do and what to avoid.
- **first-pass-profile.md** — Your voice profile: tone, rhythm, vocabulary patterns.
- **examples.md** — Before/after examples showing generic vs. your-voice rewrites.

## Building Your Voice Pack

If starting from scratch, ask your AI agent:

> "Read my recent writing samples (LinkedIn posts, emails, docs) and build a voice profile. Identify my tone, sentence structure patterns, vocabulary preferences, and anti-patterns to avoid."

Then review and refine the generated profile. The voice pack improves over time as you add more examples.
