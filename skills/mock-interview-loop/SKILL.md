# Mock Interview Loop

## Purpose

Simulate interview rounds, score performance, and improve answers quickly through iteration.

## Use When

- Preparing for recruiter, hiring manager, or panel interviews.
- Practicing behavioral and technical-execution narratives.
- Running fast readiness checks before interview day.

## Workflow

1. Define round type and likely question themes.
2. Ask one question at a time and collect answer.
3. Score answer on clarity, structure, evidence, and seniority signal.
4. Provide upgrade suggestions and revised answer.
5. Repeat until target score is reached.

## Scoring Rubric (1-5)

- Clarity
- Story structure
- Evidence and metrics
- Scope and ownership signal
- Executive presence

## Required Output

- Per-question scorecard
- What to keep / what to change
- Revised answer
- Next practice focus
