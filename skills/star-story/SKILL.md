# STAR Story

## Purpose

Create compact, high-signal STAR stories that are easy to reuse across interview themes.

## Use When

- Preparing for behavioral interviews.
- Turning project notes into interview-ready narratives.
- Filling gaps in story coverage (conflict, leadership, ambiguity, failure, influence).

## Workflow

1. Capture role context and target interview level.
2. Draft STAR in 5-8 bullets.
3. Tighten to 60-90 second spoken answer.
4. Add one follow-up depth point and one metric anchor.

## Quality Checks

- Action section reflects your personal decisions.
- Result section has measurable outcomes.
- No generic claims without proof.

## Required Output

- Story title and interview theme
- STAR draft (concise)
- 60-second spoken version
- Follow-up depth prompt
