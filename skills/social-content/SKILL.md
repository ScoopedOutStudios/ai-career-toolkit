# Social Content

## Purpose

Produce concise, credible social and outreach content that drives professional engagement.

## Default Mode

Balanced output:
- Professional posts and updates.
- Outreach messages and follow-ups.
- Tone and format adapted to platform and audience.

## Use When

- Drafting LinkedIn updates, post hooks, and comments.
- Writing recruiter/HM/referral outreach messages.
- Building short nurture and follow-up sequences.

## Workflow

1. Identify channel and audience.
2. Clarify objective (inform, engage, ask, follow up).
3. Draft concise copy with concrete signal.
4. Provide 1-2 variants with different energy levels.

## Required Output

- Primary draft
- One alternate version
- Suggested CTA
- Optional follow-up message
