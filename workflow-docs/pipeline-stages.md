# Pipeline Stages Reference

Detailed definitions for each stage in the opportunity pipeline.

## Stage Definitions

### Applied

- **Entry:** You've submitted an application or made initial contact.
- **Exit criteria:** Recruiter responds to schedule a screen, OR 14 days pass with no response.
- **Actions:** Follow up at day 7 if no response. Close at day 14 with "no response" if still silent.

### Recruiter Screen

- **Entry:** Recruiter screen is scheduled or completed.
- **Exit criteria:** Advanced to HM screen, OR rejected/withdrew.
- **Actions:** Debrief immediately after the call. Note key signals and concerns.
- **Typical duration:** 1-2 weeks from application.

### HM Screen

- **Entry:** Hiring manager screen is scheduled or completed.
- **Exit criteria:** Advanced to panel, OR rejected/withdrew.
- **Actions:** Deeper company research. Prepare targeted stories for HM's focus area.
- **Typical duration:** 1-2 weeks from recruiter screen.

### Panel

- **Entry:** Full interview loop is scheduled or in progress.
- **Exit criteria:** Offer extended, OR rejected/withdrew.
- **Actions:** Full prep cycle — mock interviews, story selection, question preparation.
- **Typical duration:** 1-3 weeks from HM screen.

### Offer

- **Entry:** Verbal or written offer received.
- **Exit criteria:** Accept, decline, or negotiate.
- **Actions:** Evaluate against role thesis. Compare with other active opportunities. Use `career-guide` for tradeoff analysis.
- **Typical duration:** 1-2 weeks decision window.

### Closed

- **Entry:** Opportunity is no longer active for any reason.
- **Required:** Record close reason (rejected, withdrew, no response, declined offer).
- **Value:** Closed-reason data improves targeting over time.

## Stage Transition Tracking

For each transition, record:
- Date of transition
- Key signal that triggered the move
- Any concerns or red flags noted
- Next action and due date

## Health Indicators

| Indicator | Healthy | Warning | Action Needed |
|-----------|---------|---------|---------------|
| Applied → Recruiter Screen | >30% conversion | 15-30% | Review resume targeting and application quality |
| Recruiter → HM Screen | >50% conversion | 30-50% | Review screen performance, level calibration |
| HM → Panel | >40% conversion | 20-40% | Review HM screen stories and technical narratives |
| Panel → Offer | >30% conversion | 15-30% | Review panel prep depth and mock interview scores |
| Days in any stage | <14 days | 14-21 days | Follow up or close |
